Perspective Assistant

Start by measuring the distance from the vanishing point to the near line and from the vanishing point to the far line. You can measure at any angle you find convenient, as long as you are consistent. You can enter fractions or decimals, e.g. “1.5”, “1 1/2” or “3/2”.

Specific Point

  • 0 means the far line.
  • 1 means the near line.
  • 1/2 (or 0.5) means half way between.
  • 1 1/2 (or 1.5) means go the whole way from far to near, then 50% further.
  • -1/2 (or -0.5) means to go the whole way from near to far, then 50% further.
  • The lower limit is .
    • That's the point where objects are no longer in front of you.
    • Objects will go off the page before getting to this point.
    • If you are trying to draw a line to such a point, draw the line in the right direction and stop when you get to the edge of your paper.
